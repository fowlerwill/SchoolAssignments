PokerDice

By Will Fowler

How to play:

	-Start the game in console, select new game.
	-Enter Your Name & your opponents name.

Gameplay:
	-you are shown the dice that you currently have, choose which of these dice you would like to
	roll (enter each die individually) then when you're satisfied
	- reset the dice that you'd like to roll by entering 'r'
	- enter e to end your turn
	- you can end your turn without rolling any dice.
	- once your 3 turns are complete, the computer will compare the player's dice, 
	and announce the winner
